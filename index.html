About OFôlies+

OFôlies is a sunset lounge in Zaarour, Mount Lebanon, perched at around 1,700 meters above sea level, about 40 minutes from Beirut. Guests arrive for golden hour and stay into the night: signature cocktails, food, and DJ sets as the sun drops behind the mountains. Open from 5 PM into the night, with table reservations recommended on weekends.

Good to know+

Where is OFôlies?

In Zaarour, Mount Lebanon, around 40 minutes from Beirut.

What time should I arrive for sunset?

Between 6:00 and 6:30 PM in summer — golden hour before the sun drops behind the mountains.

Do I need a reservation?

Yes, but tables go fast on weekends book a table online.

Is there food?

Yes! salads, raw seafood, and hot bites, alongside cocktails, wine, and spirits.
